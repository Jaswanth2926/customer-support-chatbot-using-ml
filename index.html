Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book tickets online?

Booking tickets is easy! Simply select your movie, choose your preferred showtime, select your seats, and complete the payment process. You'll receive an email confirmation with your booking details.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 2 hours before the showtime. Visit the 'My Bookings' section in your account to make changes. Refunds will be processed according to our cancellation policy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Gift cards and promotional vouchers can also be used.

Are there any booking fees?

A small convenience fee applies to online bookings. This fee helps us maintain our booking platform. Premium members enjoy waived booking fees as part of their membership benefits.

What amenities are available at your theaters?

Our theaters feature state-of-the-art sound systems, comfortable seating, and concession stands. Premium theaters include recliner seats, in-seat dining service, and enhanced audio-visual technology.

Do you offer special screenings?

Yes! We offer IMAX screenings, 3D showings, and special event screenings like midnight premieres. We also host kid-friendly sessions and sensory-friendly screenings.

What are the benefits of membership?

Members enjoy exclusive benefits including discounted tickets, waived booking fees, early access to blockbuster tickets, special concession deals, and points earning on purchases. Premium members get additional perks like free upgrades and priority seating.

How do I earn and redeem reward points?

Members earn points on every purchase. Earn 1 point per dollar spent on tickets and concessions. Points can be redeemed for free tickets, concession items, or merchandise. Check your account dashboard for current point balance and redemption options.
